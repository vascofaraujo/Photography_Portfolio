About me 😃

Hi! My name is Vasco and I was born and raised in Lisbon, Portugal.

I am taking a Master's in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Técnico with a focus on AI and Robotics.

I started in August of 2020 when I bought my first film camera, a Pentax K1000. Recently I have also been shooting with an old Nikkormat that belonged to my grandfather.

My goal with photography is to have fun and capture photos I like. My main subjects are architecture and landscape

This is my first website and it is a way for me to learn the basics of web development while displaying my pictures.
